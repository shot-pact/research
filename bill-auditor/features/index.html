shot·pact · research · feature list

A mobile auditor for American medical bills.

shot-pact scans itemized bills or EOBs, flags the five canonical billing errors — duplicates, upcodes, surprise charges, unbundling, opaque lump sums — drafts a dispute letter citing the No Surprises Act, and sends it through certified mail, the hospital patient portal, or email. Pricing is contingency-based: free unless we recover money for you.

04

Flag taxonomy

Every flag carries a severity (high / medium / low), a confidence score (0–1), a plain-English justification, and a dollar-denominated recoverable amount.

Flag Tint Detection signal Example
Duplicate ember Same CPT billed twice in the same encounter window 99285 × 2 at same timestamp
Upcode ember Billed complexity exceeds what chart notes support 99285 where notes support 99284
Out-of-network ember OON provider at in-network facility (No Surprises Act) Anesthesiologist OON at IN hospital
Unbundled amber Line item billed separately from its global package Post-op visit outside surgical global
Opaque amber Non-itemized lump sum with no breakdown "Supplies & misc · $684.00"
