04 Finalized feature list to dominate the market
Killer — India-first, zero competitors
India-specific — required, missing everywhere
Core — must have on launch day
Phase 2 — after first 100 users
A — Lead capture & booking
A1
Multi-event booking — one contract, all 5 events
Killer
A single booking holds Mehendi, Haldi, Sangeet, Ceremony, and Reception as linked events — each with its own date, time, venue, and equipment notes. One invoice. One client portal. This is how Indian weddings work and zero CRMs support it today.
A2
Lead pipeline (kanban board)
Core
Inquiry → Proposal Sent → Contract Signed → Advance Paid → Active → Delivered. Drag cards between stages. Auto-nudge when a lead sits idle for 7 days. See every opportunity at a glance. Never lose a warm lead again.
A3
Pre-built Indian event types
India
Dropdown: Mehendi · Haldi · Sangeet · Baraat · Pheras / Ceremony · Reception · Engagement · Ring Ceremony · Pre-wedding Shoot · Post-wedding Shoot. Smart defaults (ceremony = 3 hrs, reception = 4 hrs). No more typing "Event Type 2".
A4
Embeddable inquiry form (web + WhatsApp link)
Core
Embed on any website or share as a short WhatsApp link. Captures name, wedding dates, event types, location, budget range, referral source. Auto-WhatsApp acknowledgement within 60 seconds. Every missed call becomes a captured lead.
B — Contracts & proposals
B1
Magic link e-signature — zero login required
Killer
Client gets a unique link via WhatsApp or email. Opens in browser. Signs by drawing or typing. No account creation. No password. Legally binding with IP address + timestamp + device. Directly solves HoneyBook's #1 complaint: "clients have to log in every time just to sign."
B2
Indian wedding contract templates
India
Pre-built with Indian-specific clauses: rescheduling for monsoon or family emergencies, venue change policy, copyright ownership, cancellation terms in INR, per-event delivery timelines. Fill in names and dates — everything else is handled. Saves 3+ hours per booking.
B3
Visual tiered proposal (3 packages)
Core
Show Basic / Standard / Premium side by side with event coverage, photo counts, deliverables, and prices. Client clicks their tier. Contract auto-generates. No quoting back and forth. Photographers with tiered pricing see 30–50% higher average booking values.
B4
Second shooter contract
Core
Lightweight contract: pay rate per event, copyright clause, image delivery obligation, 72-hour cancellation penalty. Auto-populates event details from the parent booking. Solves "second shooter cancelled 3 days before with no contract and no recourse."
C — Payments (biggest gap in the entire market)
C1
UPI QR code embedded in every invoice
Killer
Client opens invoice → scans QR → pays via GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, or any UPI app → payment auto-reconciles to the booking in under 10 seconds. No payment gateway required for basic flows. This single feature will make every Indian photographer switch. Zero competitors have it.
C2
Custom installment schedule with auto-reminders
Killer
Set any schedule: 30% on booking → 40% one month before → 30% after delivery. Each due date auto-sends WhatsApp + email reminder 3 days prior. Auto-charges saved card via Razorpay on the due date. Photographer never chases a payment again. The most-requested feature globally — finally solved for India.
C3
Razorpay (cards · netbanking · EMI)
India
For clients who prefer card or netbanking. 2% + GST. Auto-marks invoice paid. Supports EMI for high-value bookings — couples booking ₹2L+ can split into 6-month instalments. Real conversion booster for premium packages.
C4
INR + GST invoicing
India
All amounts in ₹. Auto-calculates 18% GST. Invoice PDF is GST-compliant with GSTIN field. Monthly GST summary report exportable for CA. No USD confusion, no manual conversion, no separate accounting tool needed for basic compliance.
C5
Cash / cheque payment logging
India
Many Indian clients still pay cash advances or by cheque. Log offline payments, upload a photo of the cheque or receipt, mark as paid. Full audit trail even for cash transactions. Log from mobile in 30 seconds at the client meeting.
C6
Payment status dashboard
Core
One screen: total collected this month, total pending, upcoming due dates this week. Red flag on any payment overdue by 7+ days. One-tap WhatsApp reminder button next to each overdue item. Full financial picture without opening a spreadsheet.
D — Client communication
D1
WhatsApp automated reminders at every milestone
Killer
Auto-send WhatsApp messages: contract sent → payment due in 3 days → 1 week before each event → shot list request → "Your gallery is ready." 58.6% of Indian couples use WhatsApp for ALL wedding communication (WeddingWire India 2025). Every reminder sent = one less follow-up call.
D2
Magic link client portal — no login, no password
Killer
One link showing contract status, invoice history, event timelines, shot list, gallery link, and questionnaire answers. No account. No password. Works on any Android phone. Designed for non-tech-savvy Indian families — parents, in-laws, and couples stay informed without calling the photographer.
D3
Emails from photographer's own domain
Core
Connect Gmail or custom domain. Emails appear from photographer@yourstudio.com — not noreply@crm.com. Directly eliminates the spam folder problem that costs HoneyBook users potential clients. Professional, trusted, on-brand.
D4
Automated email workflow per booking type
Core
Wedding booking auto-triggers: confirmation → pre-wedding questionnaire (3 months before) → shot list reminder (1 week before each event) → day-of timeline (2 days before) → delivery notification → review request. Fully automated. Zero manual emails after setup.
E — Shoot day management
E1
Shot list builder per Indian event type
India
Pre-built lists for every event: Mehendi (henna close-ups, family groups, candid laughter), Ceremony (7 pheras, sindoor moment, mangalsutra), Reception (first dance, cake cut, couple portraits). Photographer customizes and shares with client for approval. Client adds family photo requests.
E2
Day-of timeline builder
Core
Hour-by-hour timeline: 3:00 PM bridal prep → 4:00 PM Baraat arrival → 4:30 PM Jaimala → 5:00 PM family photos. Share with second shooter and client via WhatsApp. Everyone aligned. No "where should I be?" messages on wedding day.
E3
Second shooter — invite, access & payment
Core
Invite second shooter to see event details, timeline, shot list — without seeing client financials. Accept via link. Auto-WhatsApp reminder 24 hours before. Log agreed pay, track if paid. Profit per booking = total collected minus second shooter cost, calculated automatically.
E4
Vendor coordination
Phase 2
Add videographer, drone operator, and makeup artist to the same booking. Share relevant timelines with each. Track separate payment status. One view of every vendor for a multi-day Indian wedding. Critical for photographers coordinating large production teams.
F — Photo delivery
F1
Gallery locked until final payment received
Killer
Photographer uploads gallery link → system checks if balance is fully paid → if yes, client accesses instantly → if no, client sees "Complete your payment to unlock your gallery" with a one-tap payment button. Auto-WhatsApp notification sent. Permanently ends the "deliver first, chase for weeks" problem.
F2
Per-event delivery tracking
Core
Track delivery status per event: ✓ Mehendi delivered Jan 12 · ✓ Sangeet delivered Jan 18 · ⏳ Ceremony — editing (ETA Jan 28). Client sees live status in their portal. Cuts "when are my photos?" WhatsApp messages by 90%.
F3
Simple gallery link delivery (no platform switch)
Core
Paste any link: Google Drive, WeTransfer, Pixieset, Dropbox. Client notified via WhatsApp + email. Auto-expires after 90 days. No need to migrate to a new gallery tool — use whatever you already use, delivered properly through the CRM.
F4
Face-recognition guest gallery
Phase 2
Guests scan a QR code, take a selfie, instantly see every photo they appear in. 300+ guests each finding and sharing their own photos = massive social reach = free marketing for the photographer at every wedding. Premium upsell at ₹5,000–10,000 per booking.
G — Business management
G1
Revenue dashboard
Core
This month's collections, pending receivables, year-to-date total, booking count, average booking value, lead-to-booking conversion rate. One screen. No exports. No spreadsheet. Solves the "finance tracking leaves something to be desired" complaint in every existing CRM's reviews.
G2
Booking calendar with Google Calendar sync
Core
Visual calendar showing every shoot across every event. Color-coded by event type. Two-way Google Calendar sync. Block personal dates. See free weekends at a glance. Double-booking is the single event that can destroy a photographer's reputation — this prevents it entirely.
G3
Android-first mobile app
India
85%+ of Indian photographers use Android. App: capture an inquiry from a phone call in 30 seconds, send a payment reminder with one tap, view today's shoot timeline and shot list, log a cash payment on the go. Partial offline support for shoot-day use without WiFi.
G4
Automated review collection
Phase 2
7 days after gallery delivery, auto-send WhatsApp + email requesting a review on Google, WedMeGood, or WeddingWire India. One-tap direct link. 52% of Indian couples find vendors via word-of-mouth — online reviews amplify this. Set once, runs forever for every booking.
